Rental Agreements

Rental Agreements define who rents what for how much and over what period of time. Rental Agreements have one or more Payors and one or more Users. Payors are financially responsible for the rent. Users are those who have access to whatever is being rented. There can be one or more Rentables (rented items) associated with the Rental Agreement.

May 7, 2019 Update History In the update released on in the Actions area any attempt to change the state of a Rental Agreement that is in the Terminated state will not be allowed and will cause an error message to be posted.
May 9, 2019 Update History When you click Edit on an Active rental agreement, the dates in the edited form are set as follows:
  • If the rental agreement's stop date is in the past or its start date is in the future then the form's new dates will be loaded with the dates of the active agreement.
  • If the rental agreement is active, the start date will be set to the current system date.
